3-Week Game Jam Project

Technical Art Portfolio

During this project, I worked on several technical and artistic aspects of the game, including VFX, shaders, post-processing, lighting, UI integration, scripting, optimization, and asset integration. My role was to improve gameplay readability while enhancing the visual atmosphere and overall consistency of the project.

What I Contributed to the Project

A quick overview of the main responsibilities I handled during production.

⚡
Gameplay VFX

Created several gameplay-oriented visual effects to improve player feedback, including an electrical effect for circuit breakers, a smoke effect triggered when the monster is flashed, and celebration effects linked to the machine reaching 100% charge.

◈
Rendering & Shaders

Developed an outline shader to highlight interactable objects and improve gameplay readability. I also created a vintage television post-process effect inspired by 1930s cinema to reinforce immersion, as well as HDRP Custom Passes used for both the outline system and the flashlight rendering setup.

⌘
Scripting

I developed and modified several scripts to integrate my systems into the game. I developed the flashlight battery system and a lightOnOff script that allows a group of lights to be turned off and made to blink with a flickering effect. I also assisted the programmer in implementing additional gameplay features.

☰
UI Integration

Integrated the user interface, including menu management, animated transitions, settings menus, input mapping displays, automatic keyboard/gamepad switching, and the quest system.

◆
Asset Integration

Integrated various assets into the project, adapted them to the game environment, adjusted their visual settings, and ensured they matched the overall artistic direction and atmosphere.

☠
Monster Animation Integration

Integrated the monster's animation sets into Unity, configured the Animator state machine, and connected animations with gameplay and AI behaviors through code.

▱
Optimization

Optimized the scene by creating LOD setups for meshes and implementing culling systems across the level to improve overall performance.

✦
Lighting & Atmosphere

Responsible for the complete lighting setup and visual atmosphere of the game. The goal was to create a dark horror environment while ensuring players could still navigate the map comfortably and maintain good gameplay readability.

My Technical Artist Approach

Connecting artistic intent, technical constraints, and gameplay feedback.

01 Gameplay

Identify what the player needs to understand immediately.

02 Visual Design

Create a visual effect, lighting setup, or UI element that makes the action clear and readable.

03 Technical Implementation

Integrate everything cleanly into Unity with flexible systems that are easy to iterate on and maintain.
